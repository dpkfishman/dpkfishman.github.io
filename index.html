David Fishman is a sophomore at Northwestern University studying journalism and politics. In Washington, D.C., he is reporting on Politics for the Medill News Service. On campus, David writes and edits for The Daily Northwestern and is a member of the Phi Delta Theta fraternity. David previously interned for Engadget and has had work published in USA Today, New York Daily News, MarketWatch, U.S. News & World Report, Time Out, GQ and The Telegraph.
